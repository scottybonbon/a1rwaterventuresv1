The company

We don't find water. We create it.

Mission & vision

A new source for the world's water.

Mission

We are on a mission to redefine the water we drink. We refuse to accept that exceptional hydration has to come from the ground — so we capture it directly from the air, a limitless and pristine source that leaves our rivers, wells, and aquifers untouched and our oceans unburdened. Our mission is to lead a new era of conscious luxury: delivering an unrivaled drinking experience that sets a new standard for what water can — and should — mean. Exceptional water. Exceptional responsibility.

Vision

A world where the sky is the source — where pure, sustainable hydration flows freely without burdening the Earth, and true luxury is defined not by what we take, but by what we protect.

Our values

What we will not compromise.

01

Be responsible

Every decision traces back to a single question: does this protect the source? We will not compromise on where our water comes from, or what it costs the Earth.

02

Earn trust

Investors, partners, and consumers will hold us to the highest standard. We welcome that. Transparency is not a disclosure requirement; it is how we build the confidence that sustains long-term value.

03

Hold high standards

Responsible doesn't mean ordinary. We hold every product, every experience, and every touchpoint to the highest standard, because the case for conscious luxury only works if the luxury is real.

04

Think limitlessly

The atmosphere is everywhere. So is our ambition. We operate with the conviction that scarcity is a failure of imagination, and that the right source, scaled correctly, changes everything.
